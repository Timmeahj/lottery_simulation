Lottery simulation

The maximum amount of times you can run multiple lottery draws with this application is 1 million. If you choose to run it more than that it will limit to 1 million. Since the total amount of combinations you can make, regardless of order is around 8 million (8145060) most of the time you'll find one specific combination after running the maximum 8 times.
